Kenya Mukimo Recipe

This recipe is got from govisitkenya.com website (How to Prepare Kenya Mukimo Recipe

Description

Kenya Mukimo Recipe is a traditional Kikuyu dish. It is served at all major ceremonies especially weddings, funerals and fund raisings. A celebration is incomplete without this delicacy.

Ingredients

  • Fresh green maize (shelled)
  • Young tender leaves of a pumpkin and/or Spinach.
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Irish Potatoes
  • Butter or margarine
  • 3 cups of water

Steps

  1. Boil green maize for at least two hours or until they are cooked.
  2. Add the peeled and diced potatoes but do not mix. Place the thoroughly washed pumpkin leaves on top of the potatoes.
  3. Sprinkle some salts.
  4. Let the mixture boil for another 30 minutes to 45 minutes until the potatoes and the greens are well cooked. Remember, never stir this mixture otherwise you will make a mess out of your Mukimo.
  5. Once satisfied that the maize, potatoes and greens are well cooked, drain the remaining water and let the mixture simmer under low heat.
  6. Mix and mash the mixture. Add some margarine to give the Mukimo a delicate flavour. Ensure that the maize, potatoes and pumpkin leaves are evenly spread.
  7. The final product should have a very fine texture, is light green in colour and should neither be dry nor very moist. Some people like it when the maize remains intact(whole).
